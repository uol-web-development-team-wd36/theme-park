Lodgings

Grand Pyriot Hotel

Looking to stay in luxury? Money not an issue? Well look no further than the Grand Pyriot Hotel, located only a 5 minute drive from our theme park, the Grand Pyriot Hotel is home to world class service. Featuring stunning views right from your bedroom window, and many luxurious features, this hotel is hailed as one of the top in the world.

This hotel includes:

  • 5 restaurants, including greek, italian, spanish, chinese and Continental Buffet
  • A gym, with all your workout needs
  • A luxury pool and poolside bar
  • A variety of shops
  • Pick up and drop off transportation
  • In room service
  • Laundry and dry cleaning service
  • free wifi
  • Daily Activities and tours

Little Cottage Suites

If you want to stay somewhere that feels more like home, then this is for you. The Little Cottage Suites pride themselves on providing a cozy and authentic experience for guests. Located in the old part of town, this place will have you reminiscing of the past. A 10 minute drive, or 20 minute bus ride from the them park.

This hotel includes:

  • Breakfast
  • Pick up and drop off transportation
  • Laundry and dry cleaning service
  • Free wifi
  • Located in the old part of town
  • Private kitchen and living room

The Hampton

Looking for value for your money? Then The Hampton is for you. This 4 star hotel is both affordable and luxurious, providing all the regular utilities and hotel services you could want at an affordable price. Located a 10 minute drive or 15 minute underground ride from our theme park.

This hotel includes:

  • 2 restaurants, including a Continental Buffet
  • A gym, for all your workout needs
  • Pick up and drop off transportation
  • In room service
  • Laundry and dry cleaning service
  • Free wifi
  • Located right next to harbour street, famous for all its upmarket shopping
